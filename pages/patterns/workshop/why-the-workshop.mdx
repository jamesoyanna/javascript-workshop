# Why the Workshop
Today companies are looking for exceptional software engineers who can interpret their business problem into an actual software application.

 An exceptional engineer is a reliable team player trusted by a company to handle confidential business problems. 
 
 They excel in collaboration,possess strong technical communication skills, and have the ability to turn business problem (logic) into real-world applications seamlessly.

 In this workshop, you will learn the best practise in software development in the areas of:

 - Your approach to solving engineering problems.

 - Technical communication

 - Collaboration (Pair programming / Code Reviews)
